About me

I am a maître de conférences at Université Paris 8 and a member of the PASTIS team.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in combinatorics and its interactions with computer science and physics. I've worked on various subjects ranging from graph theory to algorithmics, logic, and even quantum computing.

My thesis was on the combinatorics of the linear λ-calculus, maps (as in graphs drawn on surfaces), and their interplay.

News

  • August 2025: Together with Noam Zeilberger and Olivier Bodini, we published "Asymptotic distribution of parameters in trivalent maps and linear lambda terms" in Combinatorial Theory.
  • June 2025: Aboubacar Sidikou Rabiou Issoufou has completed his L3 internship with me on the subject of comonads, their compositions, and distributive laws.
